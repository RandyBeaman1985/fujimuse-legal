FujiMuse Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 28, 2026

Introduction

FujiMuse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, display name, profile picture, bio, and camera model when you create an account
  • User Content: Photos you upload, recipes you create, posts, comments, and captions
  • AI Chat Content: Messages you choose to send through our AI chat feature and the AI responses returned to you
  • Purchase Tokens: Google Play purchase tokens used to verify paid feature access. We do not receive your payment card details.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Features you use, actions you take, time and frequency of activities
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics is disabled by default and collects aggregated usage events only after you grant consent in the app.
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics is disabled by default and collects crash logs only after you grant consent. Crash data does not include your personal content such as messages, photos, or recipe details.

Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers: If you sign in with a third-party service, we receive your profile information from that service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FujiMuse app
  • Process your transactions and subscriptions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Provide AI-powered recipe suggestions and chat responses
  • Generate recipe previews using AI image processing
  • Display your content to other users (posts, comments, profile)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

AI Features and Data Processing

AI Chat

  • Messages you send to the AI assistant are routed through our Supabase Edge Function proxy to third-party AI services for processing
  • FujiMuse currently uses Google Gemini as the primary AI chat provider and may use Anthropic Claude as a fallback
  • When you use AI chat, your text messages and selected camera context may be sent to the AI provider through our server-side proxy
  • Your photos are never sent to the AI chat service
  • Chat history is stored locally on your device so the conversation can continue in the app. It is not persisted to our Supabase database.
  • Provider retention and training terms are governed by the providers' commercial API terms
  • AI-generated recipe suggestions are creative guidance, not guaranteed camera or vendor facts, and should be verified before use

Recipe Preview

  • When you generate an AI-enhanced visual preview, your uploaded photo is sent through our server-side proxy to Replicate along with recipe parameters
  • Images are processed by third-party AI models for style transfer. On-device LUT preview does not transmit photos.
  • Generated previews are temporarily cached and may be stored in your post history

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With Service Providers: Cloud hosting and auth (Supabase), AI chat processing (Google Gemini primary, Anthropic Claude fallback), AI image preview processing (Replicate), payment processing (Google Play Billing), and optional analytics/crash reporting after consent (Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics)
  • With Other Users: Your username, display name, profile picture, bio, posts, photos, recipe data, and comments are publicly visible to other users as part of the social features. Your email address is never displayed to other users.
  • For Legal Reasons: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Posts and Content: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • Chat History: Stored locally on your device until you clear it, delete the app data, or delete your account
  • Telemetry: Retained for 14 months in Firebase only if you opt in
  • Crash Reports: Retained for 90 days in Crashlytics only if you opt in
  • Purchase Tokens: Retained for entitlement, tax, accounting, refund, and fraud-prevention needs

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your recipes and content in a portable format (JSON)
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics and crash reporting consent in settings
