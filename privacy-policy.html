FujiMuse Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Introduction

FujiMuse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, username, display name, profile picture, bio, and camera model when you create an account
  • User Content: Photos you upload, recipes you create, posts, comments, and captions
  • Communications: Messages you send through our AI chat feature

Information Collected Automatically

  • Usage Data: Features you use, actions you take, time and frequency of activities
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Log Data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent
  • Analytics Data: Firebase Analytics collects aggregated usage events such as screens viewed, recipes created, posts liked, and feature engagement. Analytics data is anonymized and used to improve the app experience.
  • Crash Reports: Firebase Crashlytics collects crash logs, stack traces, device state at time of crash, and a hashed user identifier. Crash data does not include your personal content such as messages, photos, or recipe details. Crash reporting is disabled in debug builds and only active in production releases.

Information from Third Parties

  • Authentication Providers: If you sign in with a third-party service, we receive your profile information from that service

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the FujiMuse app
  • Process your transactions and subscriptions
  • Send you technical notices and support messages
  • Respond to your comments and questions
  • Provide AI-powered recipe suggestions and chat responses
  • Generate recipe previews using AI image processing
  • Display your content to other users (posts, comments, profile)
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve our services
  • Detect and prevent fraud or abuse

AI Features and Data Processing

AI Chat

  • Messages you send to the AI assistant are processed by third-party AI services
  • When you use the AI chat, your text messages and your selected camera model are sent to third-party servers for processing
  • Your photos are never sent to the AI chat service
  • Conversations may be stored locally to provide context within sessions and are cleared after 30 days of inactivity
  • We do not use your chat history to train AI models
  • AI-generated recipe suggestions and responses may not be accurate and should be verified before use

Recipe Preview

  • When you generate a visual preview, your uploaded photo is sent to a third-party AI API along with recipe parameters
  • Images are processed by third-party AI models for style transfer
  • Generated previews are temporarily cached and may be stored in your post history

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information:

  • With Service Providers: Cloud hosting (Supabase), AI services, payment processing, analytics and crash reporting (Google Firebase Analytics, Firebase Crashlytics)
  • With Other Users: Your username, display name, profile picture, bio, posts, photos, recipe data, and comments are publicly visible to other users as part of the social features. Your email address is never displayed to other users.
  • For Legal Reasons: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active
  • Posts and Content: Retained until you delete them or your account
  • Chat History: Session-based, cleared after 30 days of inactivity
  • Usage Logs: Retained for 90 days for security and analytics

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data
  • Portability: Export your recipes in standard formats
  • Opt-out: Disable analytics tracking in settings

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@fujimuse.app

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures including:

  • Encryption in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Encryption at rest for sensitive data
  • Secure authentication with JWT tokens
  • Row-level security for database access
  • Regular security audits

Children's Privacy

FujiMuse is not intended for children under 13 years of age and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@fujimuse.app. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly. Users between 13 and 18 should review this policy with a parent or guardian.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy and updating the "Last Updated" date.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

We do not sell personal information as defined by the CCPA.

European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights including:

  • Right to access, rectification, and erasure
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing
  • Right to withdraw consent

Our legal basis for processing is:

  • Contract: To provide our services
  • Consent: For optional features and marketing
  • Legitimate Interest: For security and service improvement
